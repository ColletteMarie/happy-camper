Hiring Guide

Campervanning is a great way to travel around and discover new places. It gives you the freedom to explore far and wide with the added bonus of taking your creature comforts with you.

Here are some frequently asked questions, hints and tips to help get you started on your campervan journey.

Who can hire a campervan?

To hire a campervan you must hold a clean and valid UK driving lisence and be 21 or over.

What type of campervan should I hire?

There are a number of different campervans available. You should think about how many people will be in your group, where you're going and how long you're going away for.

What items will be provided with my campervan hire?

Each campervan comes equipped with:

  • Bedding
  • Kitchenware such as cuttlery, cooking utensils, plates, bowls and cups
  • Tea towels x2
  • Radio
  • Sat nav

Our campers also have power sockets but you will need to bring your own power cables for your electronic devices, such as phone chargers.

Please tell us if you're travelling with children as we can provide bumper seats if required, however we don't provide car seats for babies.

Can you recommend campervan-friendly sites?

We certainly can. Have a look at our list of recommended campasites.

Can I take the campervan abroad?

Sorry, our campervan's are only insured for driving in the UK.

Can I talk to someone about the different campervan options available to me?

Of course, just get in touch. You can call us on 020 1234 5678. You can also email hello@happycamper.co.uk
